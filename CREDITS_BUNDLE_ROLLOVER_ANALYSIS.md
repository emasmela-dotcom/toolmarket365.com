# 💳 Credits Bundle + Rollover Analysis - $10 per 100 Credits

## 🎯 **Your Proposal**

**Pricing:**
- $10 per 100 credits
- Credits roll over month to month (don't expire)

**Let's analyze this!**

---

## 💡 **Why This is Smart**

### **1. Bundle Pricing:**
- $10 for 100 credits = $0.10 per credit
- But buying in bulk feels better
- Encourages larger purchases

### **2. Rollover Feature:**
- Credits don't expire
- Users can stockpile
- Creates "sunk cost" psychology
- Encourages usage (they want to use what they paid for)

## 🤔 **Potential Issues with Rollover**

### **Issue 1: Stockpiling**
```
User buys 100 credits
Uses 10 credits/month
Has 90 credits rolling over
Might not buy more for 9 months
```

**Solution:**
- ✅ They already paid $10
- ✅ Creates long-term engagement
- ✅ They'll eventually use them
- ✅ Or encourage usage with promotions

### **Issue 2: Free Credits + Rollover**
```
User has 25 free credits/month
Buys 100 credits
Uses 20 credits/month
Credits keep accumulating
```

**Solution:**
- ✅ Cap rollover at reasonable amount (e.g., 500 credits max)
- ✅ Or let them accumulate (they paid for them!)
- ✅ Encourage usage with "use it or lose it" after 6 months

---

## 💡 **Recommended Structure**

### **Bundle Pricing:**
```
25 credits: $2.50 (same rate)
50 credits: $4.50 (save $0.50)
100 credits: $10.00 (best value) ⭐
200 credits: $18.00 (save $2.00)
```

### **Rollover Rules:**
```
✅ Free credits: Reset monthly (don't roll over)
✅ Purchased credits: Roll over indefinitely
✅ Or: Cap at 500 credits max
✅ Or: Expire after 6 months (use it or lose it)
```
