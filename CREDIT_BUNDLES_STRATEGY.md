# 💳 Credit Bundles Strategy - Recommendations

## 🎯 **Current State**
- Single option: $10 for 100 credits ($0.10 per credit)
- Tool costs: 5-15 credits per use
- Free credits: 25/month per plan

---

## ✅ **YES - Create Bundles! Here's Why:**

### **1. Psychology & Value Perception**
- ✅ **Anchoring effect**: Large bundle makes smaller bundles look reasonable
- ✅ **Volume discount feeling**: "I'm getting a deal!"
- ✅ **Reduces friction**: Less decision-making for users
- ✅ **Professional appearance**: Multiple options = established business

### **2. Revenue Optimization**
- ✅ **Higher average order value**: Users buy more than they need
- ✅ **Upsell opportunity**: "For just $5 more, get 50 extra credits!"
- ✅ **Reduces transaction costs**: Fewer purchases = less processing fees
- ✅ **Cash flow**: Larger upfront payments

### **3. User Experience**
- ✅ **Flexibility**: Different users have different needs
- ✅ **Entry point**: Small bundle for testing
- ✅ **Power user option**: Large bundle for heavy users
- ✅ **Less "nickel and diming"**: Feels more premium
