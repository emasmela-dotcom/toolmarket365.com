# 💰 Domain Pricing: Vercel vs GoDaddy

## **Quick Answer:**

**If you buy through Vercel:**
- ✅ You get **standard registrar pricing** (usually Namecheap rates)
- ✅ **Same price** as buying directly from the registrar partner
- ✅ **No markup** - Vercel doesn't charge extra
- ❌ **May not get** GoDaddy's promotional first-year deals

**If you buy through GoDaddy:**
- ✅ Often has **promotional first-year pricing** ($0.99-$2.99 for first year)
- ⚠️ **Higher renewal rates** ($17.99+/year after first year)
- ⚠️ **Upsells and add-ons** during checkout
- ⚠️ **Manual DNS setup** required

---

## 📊 **Pricing Breakdown**

### **Vercel Domain Purchase:**
- **Partner:** Usually Namecheap
- **.com pricing:** ~$10-15/year (standard rate)
- **First year:** Same as renewal (no promo)
- **Renewal:** Same price (consistent)
- **WHOIS Privacy:** Included free
- **No upsells:** Clean checkout

### **GoDaddy Domain Purchase:**
- **First year promo:** $0.99-$2.99 (very cheap!)
- **Renewal rate:** $17.99+/year (much higher)
- **WHOIS Privacy:** ~$10/year extra
- **Upsells:** Hosting, email, security (during checkout)

## 📝 **Alternative: Best of Both Worlds**

If you really want GoDaddy's first-year promo:

1. **Buy domain from GoDaddy** ($0.99 first year)
2. **Add to Vercel** (Settings → Domains)
3. **Add DNS records** manually (5 minutes)
4. **Save $10-12 in year 1**
5. **Transfer to Vercel/Namecheap** after year 1 (to avoid $17.99 renewal)

**Transfer cost:** ~$12 (one-time)
**Still saves money** if you transfer after year 1
