## 🌍 **International Fees (Non-US Cards)**

### **Stripe:**
- US cards: 2.9% + 30¢
- International cards: 3.9% + 30¢
- **Example:** $10 transaction
  - US card: $0.59 fee
  - International: $0.69 fee

### **Paddle:**
- All cards: 5% + 50¢ (flat rate)
- **Example:** $10 transaction = $1.00 fee

### **LemonSqueezy:**
- All cards: 3.5% + 30¢ (flat rate)
- **Example:** $10 transaction = $0.65 fee

### **PayPal:**
- US: 2.9% + 30¢
- International: 4.4% + fixed fee (varies by country)
- **Example:** $10 transaction
  - US: $0.59 fee
  - UK: ~$0.74 fee

## 💡 **Hidden Costs to Consider**

### **Stripe:**
- ✅ No monthly fee
- ✅ No setup fee
- ❌ You handle VAT/taxes (time cost)
- ❌ Chargeback fee: $15 per chargeback
- ❌ Payout delay: 2-7 days

**Total Cost:** Fees + your time for tax handling

---

### **Paddle:**
- ✅ No monthly fee
- ✅ No setup fee
- ✅ They handle VAT/taxes (saves you time)
- ✅ No chargeback fees (they handle it)
- ✅ Faster payouts (1-2 days)

**Total Cost:** Just the 5% fee (everything included)

---

### **LemonSqueezy:**
- ✅ No monthly fee
- ✅ No setup fee
- ✅ They handle taxes
- ✅ Built-in affiliate system (saves you money)
- ⚠️ Chargeback handling: Varies

**Total Cost:** 3.5% fee + tax handling included

## 📈 **Break-Even Analysis**

### **When Does Paddle Make Sense?**

**Paddle costs more, but saves you time on taxes.**

**Break-even calculation:**
- Paddle: 5% fee
- Stripe: 2.9% fee
- **Difference:** 2.1% extra

**If you value your time at $50/hour:**
- Paddle costs $105/month more at $5,000 revenue
- If tax handling takes >2 hours/month, Paddle is worth it

**Verdict:** Paddle makes sense if:
- You're expanding internationally (UK/EU)
- Tax handling takes >2 hours/month
- You value simplicity over cost
