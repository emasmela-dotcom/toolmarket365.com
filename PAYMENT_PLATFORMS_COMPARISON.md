# 💳 Payment Platforms Comparison - Stripe vs Alternatives

## 🎯 **Quick Answer: Stripe is Still Best, But Here Are Alternatives**

**Stripe is the industry standard** for SaaS subscriptions, but there are alternatives depending on your needs.

---

## 📊 **Market Share & Usage**

### **Most Popular Payment Platforms (2026):**

1. **Stripe** - 60%+ of SaaS companies
2. **PayPal** - 40%+ (but often used alongside Stripe)
3. **Square** - 15% (mainly US, retail-focused)
4. **Paddle** - 10% (SaaS-focused, handles VAT/taxes)
5. **LemonSqueezy** - 5% (newer, growing fast)
6. **Adyen** - 5% (enterprise-focused)

**Verdict:** Stripe is most popular, but alternatives exist for specific needs.

---

## 🔍 **Detailed Platform Comparison**

### **1. Stripe** ⭐ **CURRENT / RECOMMENDED**

**Market Share:** #1 (60%+ of SaaS)
**Best For:** Most SaaS businesses, international expansion

**Pros:**
- ✅ **Most popular** - Industry standard
- ✅ **Best documentation** - Easiest to integrate
- ✅ **International** - Supports 40+ countries, 135+ currencies
- ✅ **Subscription management** - Built-in recurring billing
- ✅ **Developer-friendly** - Great API, webhooks, dashboard
- ✅ **VAT handling** - Automatic VAT calculation
- ✅ **Fraud protection** - Built-in 3D Secure, Radar
- ✅ **Mobile support** - iOS/Android SDKs
- ✅ **Payment methods** - Cards, ACH, Apple Pay, Google Pay, etc.

**Cons:**
- ❌ **Fees:** 2.9% + 30¢ per transaction (US)
- ❌ **International fees:** 3.9% + 30¢ (non-US cards)
- ❌ **You handle VAT** - Must register and file yourself
- ❌ **Chargeback fees:** $15 per chargeback
- ❌ **Payout delays:** 2-7 days (depending on country)

**Fees:**
- US cards: 2.9% + 30¢
- International cards: 3.9% + 30¢
- ACH: 0.8% (capped at $5)
- No monthly fee

**Verdict:** ✅ **Best overall choice** - Most flexible, best support, industry standard

---

### **2. Paddle** ⭐ **BEST FOR VAT/TAX HANDLING**

**Market Share:** #4 (10% of SaaS)
**Best For:** International SaaS, want someone else to handle taxes

**Pros:**
- ✅ **VAT/Tax handling** - They handle ALL taxes globally
- ✅ **Merchant of Record** - They're the seller (you're not)
- ✅ **No tax registration needed** - They handle everything
- ✅ **Lower international fees** - Better rates for non-US
- ✅ **Built-in compliance** - GDPR, tax laws, etc.
- ✅ **Subscription management** - Good recurring billing
- ✅ **Customer support** - They handle customer billing questions

**Cons:**
- ❌ **Higher fees:** 5% + 50¢ (but includes tax handling)
- ❌ **Less flexible** - Can't customize checkout as much
- ❌ **Smaller ecosystem** - Fewer integrations
- ❌ **US market:** Not as popular in US (more EU-focused)

**Fees:**
- 5% + 50¢ per transaction
- Includes: VAT handling, tax compliance, customer support
- No monthly fee

**Verdict:** ✅ **Best if you want zero tax hassle** - Perfect for UK/EU expansion

---

### **3. LemonSqueezy** ⭐ **BEST FOR SIMPLICITY**

**Market Share:** #5 (5%, but growing fast)
**Best For:** Simple SaaS, want everything handled

**Pros:**
- ✅ **Simplest setup** - Easiest to get started
- ✅ **Merchant of Record** - Like Paddle, handles taxes
- ✅ **Built-in affiliate system** - Referral program included
- ✅ **Good documentation** - Easy to understand
- ✅ **Lower fees than Paddle:** 3.5% + 30¢
- ✅ **Good for SaaS** - Built specifically for SaaS subscriptions

**Cons:**
- ❌ **Newer platform** - Less proven than Stripe
- ❌ **Smaller ecosystem** - Fewer integrations
- ❌ **Less flexible** - Can't customize as much
- ❌ **Limited payment methods** - Cards only (no ACH, etc.)

**Fees:**
- 3.5% + 30¢ per transaction
- Includes: Tax handling, affiliate system
- No monthly fee

**Verdict:** ✅ **Best for simplicity** - If you want everything handled

---

### **4. PayPal** ⭐ **BEST FOR USER TRUST**

**Market Share:** #2 (40%+ alongside Stripe)
**Best For:** Additional payment option, user trust

**Pros:**
- ✅ **User trust** - Everyone knows PayPal
- ✅ **PayPal balance** - Users can pay from PayPal account
- ✅ **Buyer protection** - Users feel safer
- ✅ **International** - Works in 200+ countries
- ✅ **Low fees for some:** 2.9% + fixed fee (varies by country)

**Cons:**
- ❌ **Worse for subscriptions** - Not as good as Stripe
- ❌ **Account holds** - Can freeze funds
- ❌ **Worse API** - Not as developer-friendly
- ❌ **Higher chargeback risk** - More disputes
- ❌ **User experience** - Redirects to PayPal (less seamless)

**Fees:**
- US: 2.9% + 30¢
- International: 4.4% + fixed fee
- No monthly fee

**Verdict:** ⚠️ **Use as backup** - Offer alongside Stripe for user choice

---

### **5. Square** ⭐ **BEST FOR US-ONLY**

**Market Share:** #3 (15%, mainly US)
**Best For:** US-only businesses, retail + online

**Pros:**
- ✅ **Good for US** - Excellent US support
- ✅ **Retail + online** - If you have physical presence
- ✅ **Simple pricing** - 2.9% + 30¢ (flat rate)
- ✅ **Good hardware** - If you need POS systems

**Cons:**
- ❌ **US-focused** - Not great for international
- ❌ **Worse for SaaS** - More retail-focused
- ❌ **Limited international** - Fewer countries than Stripe
- ❌ **Worse API** - Not as developer-friendly

**Fees:**
- 2.9% + 30¢ (US)
- No monthly fee

**Verdict:** ❌ **Not recommended** - Stripe is better for SaaS

---

### **6. Adyen** ⭐ **BEST FOR ENTERPRISE**

**Market Share:** #6 (5%, enterprise-focused)
**Best For:** Large enterprises, high volume

**Pros:**
- ✅ **Enterprise-grade** - Built for large scale
- ✅ **Lower fees at scale** - Volume discounts
- ✅ **Global reach** - 40+ countries
- ✅ **Advanced features** - Fraud prevention, analytics

**Cons:**
- ❌ **Enterprise-focused** - Not for small businesses
- ❌ **Higher minimums** - May require volume commitments
- ❌ **Complex setup** - More complicated than Stripe
- ❌ **Worse for small SaaS** - Overkill for most

**Fees:**
- Negotiated (volume-based)
- Usually 1.5-2.5% + fixed fee
- Monthly minimums may apply

**Verdict:** ❌ **Not for you** - Too enterprise-focused
