## ❓ FAQ Highlights

**Q: Is there a free trial?**
A: Yes! All plans include a 7-day free trial. No credit card required.

**Q: Can I change plans?**
A: Yes, you can upgrade anytime. You cannot downgrade from your selected plan after trial.

**Q: Do credits expire?**
A: Free credits reset monthly. Purchased credits never expire and roll over.

**Q: What tools require credits?**
A: Premium tools like Viral Predictor, Competitor Analyzer, and Advanced Analytics. Core tools are unlimited.

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**
A: Yes, cancel anytime with no penalties. Access continues until end of billing period.
