## 🎯 **PRICING PSYCHOLOGY TIPS**

### **1. Anchor High, Sell Low**
- Show All Access ($49) first
- Then show individual packs ($12-19)
- Users think "Wow, packs are cheap!"

### **2. Bundle Discount**
- "Save $31 with All Access!"
- Makes $49 feel like a steal
- Encourages upgrades

### **3. Premium Tool Lock**
- Viral Content Predictor = Analytics Pack only
- Forces $15 purchase minimum
- Most users also need Essentials = $34 total

### **4. Social Proof**
- "Join 10,000+ creators using Analytics Pack"
- "Most popular: Essentials + Analytics = $34/month"
- Shows what others are buying

### **5. Scarcity/Urgency**
- "Limited time: 20% off annual plans"
- "First 100 users get lifetime discount"
- Creates FOMO
