## 🚨 **IDENTIFIED ISSUES**

### **ISSUE 1: Business Tools in Wrong Tier**

**Problem:**
- **Rate Calculator** and **Revenue Tracker** are in **Creator ($79)**
- These are **basic business tools** that even Essential-tier users need
- A creator "building their workflow" should track rates/revenue

**Impact:**
- Users who need business tools must pay $79/month
- Creates gap: "I need to track revenue but I'm not a professional creator yet"
- Missed revenue opportunity

**Solution:**
Move Business & Monetization tools to **Essential** or **Professional** tier:
- **Essential ($19)**: Add Rate Calculator (basic)
- **Professional ($49)**: Add Revenue Tracker (advanced)

**OR** create add-ons:
- Rate Calculator: $3/month add-on to Essential
- Revenue Tracker: $5/month add-on to Professional
