## 📊 **UK Market Overview**

### **Market Size:**
- **UK Content Creators:** ~10-15 million

- **Culture:** Different content preferences, humor, references

### **4. UK Payment Methods** ⭐ **CONVERSION CRITICAL**

**What to Do:**
- Accept GBP (£) payments
- Add UK payment methods: PayPal, Stripe (UK), bank transfer
- Show prices in GBP
- Handle VAT (20%) correctly

**Implementation:**
```
- Stripe: Enable UK payments (already supports GBP)
- Pricing: Show £9/month, £19/month, etc.
- VAT: Add 20% VAT for UK customers (or include in price)
- Checkout: UK address fields, postcodes
```

**Cost:** Stripe fees (2.9% + 20p per transaction)
**Impact:** Critical - UK users won't convert without GBP

**Implementation:**
```
- Text replacement: "favorite" → "favourite"
```

**Cost:** $0 (just text changes)
