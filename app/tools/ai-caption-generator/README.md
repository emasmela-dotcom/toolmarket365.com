# AI Caption Generator

## What It Does
Generates 3 social media captions instantly based on your image/video description, selected tone, platform, and length preference.

## How to Use

1. **Describe your content** - Paste image description or video script
2. **Select tone:**
   - Funny/Witty
   - Inspirational
   - Professional
   - Casual
   - Informative
3. **Choose platform** (Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Facebook)
4. **Pick length:**
   - Short (≤ 70 chars)
   - Medium (70-220 chars)
   - Long (220+ chars)
5. **Click "Generate 3 captions"**

## Expected Outcome

- **3 unique captions** tailored to your tone and platform
- **Platform-optimized** formatting and hashtags
- **Tone-appropriate** language and style
- **Length-optimized** for your selected preference
- **Ready to copy** and use immediately

## Tone Examples

- **Funny**: Witty, humorous, engaging
- **Inspirational**: Motivational, uplifting, empowering
- **Professional**: Business-focused, authoritative, polished
- **Casual**: Relaxed, friendly, conversational
- **Informative**: Educational, fact-based, helpful

## Tips

- Be specific in your description for better results
- Different platforms get different caption styles
- Long captions include additional context
- All captions include relevant hashtags
