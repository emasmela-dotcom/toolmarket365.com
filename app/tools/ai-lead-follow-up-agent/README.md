# AI Lead Follow-Up Agent

## What It Does
Automatically generates personalized follow-up emails for your leads. Tracks lead status, schedules follow-ups, and creates professional email templates - all without any API costs!

## How to Use

1. **Set Your Name:**
   - Enter your name at the top (used in email signatures)
   - This is saved automatically

2. **Add Leads:**
   - Click "Add Lead" button
   - Enter lead information:
     - Name (required)
     - Email (required)
     - Company (optional)
     - Source (e.g., "Brand collaboration", "Sponsored post inquiry")
     - Notes (optional)
   - Click "Add Lead"

3. **Generate Follow-Up Email:**
   - Select a lead from the list
   - Choose follow-up type:
     - **First Follow-Up** - Initial follow-up after first contact
     - **Second Follow-Up** - Second attempt
     - **Final Follow-Up** - Last attempt (respectful closure)
   - Choose tone:
     - **Professional** - Business-appropriate
     - **Friendly** - Warm and approachable
     - **Casual** - Informal and relaxed
   - Add custom message (optional) - Personal note added as P.S.
   - Click "Generate Follow-Up Email"

4. **Use the Email:**
   - Review the generated email
   - Click "Copy" to copy to clipboard
   - Paste into your email client and send
   - Click "Mark as Contacted" to update lead status

5. **Track Follow-Ups:**
   - System automatically schedules next follow-up (3 days from contact)
   - See which leads need follow-up today
   - Track follow-up count for each lead

## Lead Statuses

- **New** - Just added, not contacted yet
- **Contacted** - Initial contact made
- **Followed-Up** - Follow-up sent
- **Responded** - Lead responded (positive outcome)
- **Closed** - Lead closed (won or lost)

## Follow-Up Types

### First Follow-Up
- Sent after initial contact
- Friendly reminder
- Opens door for conversation

### Second Follow-Up
- Sent if no response to first
- Acknowledges they're busy
- Still interested but respectful

### Final Follow-Up
- Last attempt
- Respectful closure
- Won't follow up again unless they respond
