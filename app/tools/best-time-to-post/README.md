# Best Time to Post

## What It Does
Shows optimal posting times as a heat map based on platform, industry, and timezone. Highlights top-3 engagement windows for maximum reach.

## How to Use

1. **Select platform** (Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Facebook)
2. **Choose your industry:**
   - Travel & Tourism
   - Fashion & Beauty
   - Food & Restaurants
   - Education
   - Fitness & Wellness
   - Gaming
   - B2B
   - E-commerce
3. **Pick timezone** (Eastern, Central, Pacific, GMT, Central EU)
4. **View heat map** - Green cells show optimal posting times
5. **Export to .ics** - Add optimal times to your calendar

## Expected Outcome

- **Visual heat map** showing 24-hour grid across 7 days
- **Green highlights** indicate top-3 engagement windows
- **Platform-specific** recommendations based on 2026 data
- **Industry-optimized** times for your niche
- **Calendar export** (.ics file) with all optimal slots

## Reading the Heat Map

- **Gray cells** = Low engagement times
- **Green cells** = Top-3 engagement windows (best times to post)
- **Hover effect** = Brightens cells for better visibility
- **Day labels** = Mon-Sun across top
- **Hour labels** = 0:00-23:00 down the side

## Features

- **2026 data** - Based on latest engagement research
- **Multiple platforms** - Different recommendations per platform
- **Industry-specific** - Tailored to your niche
- **Timezone support** - Adjust for your location
- **Calendar export** - Add optimal times to your calendar app

## Tips

- Green times = highest engagement potential
- Export to calendar to set reminders
- Different industries have different peak times
- B2B typically performs best during work hours
- Consumer content performs best evenings/weekends
