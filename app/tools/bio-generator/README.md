# Bio Generator

## Purpose
Generate professional, optimized bios for your social media profiles. Create compelling bios that clearly communicate what you do and who you help, perfect for Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, TikTok, and other platforms.

## How to Use

1. **Enter Your Information:**
   - **What you do:** Describe your profession, skills, or services (e.g., "create content", "design websites", "write code")
   - **Who you help:** Describe your target audience or clients (e.g., "small businesses", "creators", "entrepreneurs")
   - **Emoji (optional):** Add 1-2 emojis to make your bio more engaging and eye-catching

2. **Generate Your Bio:**
   - Click "Generate" to create a single bio option
   - Click "Generate 3 Options" to get multiple variations to choose from
   - The tool uses different templates to create varied bio formats

3. **Use Your Bio:**
   - Review the generated bio(s)
   - Click "Copy" to copy a bio to your clipboard
   - Click "Use & Copy" on any option to set it as your main bio and copy it
   - Paste into your social media profile

## Expected Outcome

- Professional, well-formatted bios ready for social media
- Multiple bio variations to choose from
- Character count displayed for each bio
- Easy copy-to-clipboard functionality
- Bios optimized for engagement and clarity

## Features

- **Multiple Templates:** Uses various bio formats for different styles
- **Random Generation:** Each generation creates a unique bio
- **Multiple Options:** Generate 3 different bio variations at once
- **Emoji Support:** Optional emoji integration for visual appeal
- **Character Count:** See the length of each generated bio
- **Copy Functionality:** One-click copy to clipboard
- **Platform Ready:** Bios formatted for Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, TikTok, etc.

## Bio Templates

The generator uses various templates including:
- "{emoji} I help {who} {do}"
- "{do} | empowering {who}"
- "{emoji} {do} for {who}"
- And more variations

## Tips

- **Be Specific:** The more specific your "what you do" and "who you help" are, the better your bio will be
- **Use Emojis:** Emojis can make your bio stand out and increase engagement
- **Character Limits:** Keep in mind platform limits:
  - Instagram: 150 characters
  - Twitter/X: 160 characters
  - LinkedIn: 120 characters
  - TikTok: 80 characters
- **Generate Multiple:** Use "Generate 3 Options" to see different styles and pick your favorite
- **Test Different Emojis:** Try various emojis to see which works best for your brand
- **Update Regularly:** Refresh your bio periodically to keep it current and relevant
