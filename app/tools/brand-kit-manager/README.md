# Brand Kit Manager

## Purpose
Store and manage your brand identity elements including brand name, primary and secondary colors, and tagline. Keep your brand assets organized in one place for easy reference and consistency across all your content.

## How to Use

1. **Enter Brand Information:**
   - Enter your brand name in the "Brand name" field
   - Select your primary brand color using the color picker
   - Select your secondary brand color using the color picker
   - Enter your brand tagline

2. **Save Your Brand Kit:**
   - Click the "Save" button to store your brand kit in local storage
   - Your brand kit will be saved and displayed below

3. **Load Saved Brand Kit:**
   - Click the "Load" button to retrieve your previously saved brand kit
   - All fields will be populated with your saved values

4. **View Your Brand Kit:**
   - After saving, your brand kit will be displayed in a preview box
   - The preview shows your brand name, colors (with visual swatches), and tagline
   - The border color matches your primary brand color

## Expected Outcome

- A centralized place to store your brand identity elements
- Quick access to your brand colors, name, and tagline
- Visual preview of your brand kit with color swatches
- Persistent storage using browser local storage
- Easy reference when creating content to maintain brand consistency

## Features

- **Color Pickers:** Easy selection of primary and secondary brand colors
- **Local Storage:** Your brand kit is saved in your browser and persists between sessions
- **Visual Preview:** See your brand kit with color swatches and formatted display
- **Quick Access:** Load your saved brand kit instantly

## Tips

- Use this tool to maintain brand consistency across all your content
- Reference your brand kit when creating social media posts, graphics, or any branded content
- Update your brand kit whenever your brand identity changes
- The color pickers allow you to select any color for your brand palette
