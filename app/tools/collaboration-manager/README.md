# Collaboration Manager

## Purpose
Track and manage brand collaborations and partnerships. Organize all your collaborations in one place, including partner names, platforms, and deliverables. Keep track of active and upcoming collaborations.

## How to Use

1. **Enter Partner Name:**
   - Enter the brand name or creator name you're collaborating with
   - Example: "Nike", "@creator_name", "Brand XYZ"

2. **Select Platform:**
   - Choose the platform for the collaboration
   - Options: IG, TikTok, YouTube, Twitter, LinkedIn, or Multiple

3. **Enter Deliverables:**
   - Describe what content or deliverables are expected
   - Example: "1 reel + 3 stories", "1 YouTube video", "3 posts"

4. **Add Collaboration:**
   - Click "Add Collab" to save
   - Collaboration is stored and displayed in your list

5. **Manage Collaborations:**
   - View all your collaborations
   - Delete individual collaborations when done
   - Clear all collaborations if needed

## Expected Outcome

- Organized list of all collaborations
- Partner name, platform, and deliverables clearly displayed
- Easy management with delete functionality
- Persistent storage (saved in browser)
- Quick overview of all active collaborations

## Features

- **Multiple Collaborations:** Track unlimited collaborations
- **Platform Tracking:** See which platform each collab is for
- **Deliverable Tracking:** Remember what's expected from each collab
- **Easy Deletion:** Remove collaborations when completed
- **Local Storage:** All data persists in your browser
- **Clear Overview:** See all collaborations at a glance

## Tips

- **Be Specific:** Clearly describe deliverables to avoid confusion
- **Update Regularly:** Update or delete completed collaborations
- **Platform Clarity:** Specify platform to track where content goes
- **Organize by Status:** Consider adding status notes in deliverables
- **Regular Cleanup:** Delete old collaborations to keep list current
- **Backup:** Export important collaboration data if needed

## Use Cases

- **Brand Partnerships:** Track collaborations with brands
- **Creator Collaborations:** Manage partnerships with other creators
- **Campaign Tracking:** Keep track of campaign collaborations
- **Sponsored Content:** Organize sponsored content partnerships
- **Long-term Partnerships:** Track ongoing brand ambassador relationships
- **Event Collaborations:** Manage event-based partnerships

## Deliverable Examples

- "1 reel + 3 stories"
- "1 YouTube video (5-10 min)"
- "3 Instagram posts"
- "1 TikTok video + 1 Instagram post"
- "Blog post + social media promotion"
- "Podcast appearance + social posts"

## Best Practices

- **Clear Communication:** Ensure deliverables are clearly defined
- **Timeline Tracking:** Consider adding deadlines in deliverables
- **Status Updates:** Update collaborations as they progress
- **Documentation:** Keep contracts or agreements separate
- **Follow Through:** Complete deliverables as agreed
- **Relationship Building:** Focus on long-term partnerships
