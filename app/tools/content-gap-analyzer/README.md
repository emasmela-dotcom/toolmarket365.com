# Content Gap Analyzer

## Purpose
Identify content opportunities by comparing your topics with your competitors'. Find topics your competitors cover that you don't, helping you discover gaps in your content strategy and new content ideas.

## How to Use

1. **Enter Your Topics:**
   - List all the topics you currently create content about
   - Enter one topic per line
   - Examples: "social media tips", "content creation", "marketing strategies"

2. **Enter Competitor Topics:**
   - List all the topics your competitor creates content about
   - Enter one topic per line (same format as yours)
   - You can analyze multiple competitors by combining their topics

3. **Find Gaps:**
   - Click the "Find Gaps" button
   - The tool will compare the two lists and identify topics your competitor covers that you don't

4. **Review Results:**
   - See all content gaps listed
   - Copy the gaps to use for content planning
   - Use these gaps as content opportunities

## Expected Outcome

- A list of topics your competitor covers that you don't
- Clear identification of content gaps in your strategy
- Actionable content opportunities
- Easy copy-to-clipboard functionality
- Count of how many gaps were found

## Features

- **Line-by-Line Input:** Easy topic entry, one per line
- **Automatic Comparison:** Instantly finds topics you're missing
- **Topic Counter:** See how many topics you've entered
- **Copy Functionality:** Copy all gaps at once
- **Clear All:** Reset both fields quickly
- **Case-Insensitive:** Works regardless of capitalization

## Tips

- **Be Comprehensive:** List all your content topics for accurate analysis
- **Regular Analysis:** Compare with competitors regularly to stay competitive
- **Multiple Competitors:** Combine topics from multiple competitors for broader analysis
- **Content Planning:** Use gaps to plan your next content pieces
- **Prioritize:** Focus on high-value gaps that align with your audience
- **Track Changes:** Re-analyze periodically to see if you've closed gaps
- **Format Consistently:** Use similar formatting for both lists for best results

## Use Cases

- **Content Strategy:** Identify missing topics in your content plan
- **Competitive Analysis:** Understand what competitors cover that you don't
- **Content Ideas:** Generate new content ideas from gaps
- **SEO Opportunities:** Find keyword/topic opportunities
- **Audience Needs:** Discover topics your audience might be looking for
- **Content Calendar:** Fill gaps in your content calendar

## Example

**Your Topics:**
```
social media tips
content creation

```

**Competitor Topics:**

These are content opportunities you should consider adding to your strategy!
