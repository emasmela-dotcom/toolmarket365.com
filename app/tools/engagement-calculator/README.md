# Engagement Calculator

## What It Does
Calculates your social media engagement rate and compares it to platform benchmarks. Provides detailed breakdowns, projections, and performance ratings for Instagram, TikTok, Twitter, LinkedIn, and YouTube.

## How to Use

1. **Select your platform** (Instagram, TikTok, Twitter, LinkedIn, YouTube)
2. **Enter your metrics:**
   - Followers/Subscribers (required)
   - Likes per post average
   - Comments per post average
   - Shares per post average
   - Views (optional)
3. **Click "Calculate"**
4. **Review results:**
   - Overall engagement rate with rating
   - Breakdown by engagement type
   - Estimated reach and impressions
   - Projected engagement
   - Platform benchmarks

## Expected Outcome

- **Engagement Rate** - Overall percentage with rating (Excellent, Good, Average, Needs Improvement)
- **Detailed Breakdown:**
  - Like Rate (%)
  - Comment Rate (%)
  - Share Rate (%)
  - View Rate (%) if provided
- **Projections:**
  - Estimated Reach
  - Estimated Impressions
  - Projected Likes, Comments, Shares
- **Platform Benchmarks** - See how you compare to industry standards

## Calculation Formula

**Engagement Rate = (Likes + Comments + Shares) / Followers × 100**

- **Platform-specific benchmarks** - Accurate comparisons
- **Detailed breakdowns** - See each engagement type

## Tips

- Use average metrics from your last 10-20 posts
- Include views for video platforms (TikTok, YouTube)
- Compare to platform benchmarks to see where you stand
- Track over time to see improvement trends
- Use projections to set engagement goals
- Higher engagement rates indicate better content performance
