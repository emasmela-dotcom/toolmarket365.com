# Follower Growth Tracker

## Purpose
Plan and track your follower growth goals. Calculate how many new followers you need per day and week to reach your 30-day goal. Visualize your progress and set realistic growth targets.

## How to Use

1. **Enter Current Followers:**
   - Enter your current follower count
   - This is your starting point

2. **Set 30-Day Goal:**
   - Enter your target follower count for the next 30 days
   - Make sure it's greater than your current count
   - Set a realistic, achievable goal

3. **Track:**
   - Click the "Track" button
   - View your personalized growth plan

4. **Review Results:**
   - See total followers needed
   - Check daily and weekly targets
   - View growth rate percentage
   - Monitor progress with visual progress bar

## Expected Outcome

- **Total Needed:** Exact number of followers to gain in 30 days
- **Daily Target:** How many new followers needed per day
- **Weekly Target:** How many new followers needed per week
- **Growth Rate:** Percentage increase required
- **Progress Bar:** Visual representation of current progress toward goal
- **Summary:** Clear breakdown of what's needed to reach your goal

## Features

- **Daily Breakdown:** See exactly how many followers you need per day
- **Weekly Breakdown:** Plan weekly growth targets
- **Progress Visualization:** Visual progress bar showing how close you are to your goal
- **Growth Rate Calculation:** See the percentage increase needed
- **Realistic Planning:** Helps set achievable daily/weekly targets
- **Clear Metrics:** All key numbers displayed prominently

## Calculations

- **Total Needed:** Goal - Current
- **Daily Target:** Total Needed ÷ 30 days (rounded up)
- **Weekly Target:** Total Needed ÷ 4.3 weeks (rounded up)
- **Growth Rate:** (Total Needed ÷ Current) × 100%
- **Progress:** (Current ÷ Goal) × 100%
