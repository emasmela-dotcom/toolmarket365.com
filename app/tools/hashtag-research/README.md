# Hashtag Research Tool

## What It Does
Discovers the best hashtags for your content based on topics and keywords. Shows volume, engagement potential, competition level, and trending status. Organizes hashtags into optimal mixes, trending tags, and niche opportunities.

## How to Use

1. **Enter a topic or keyword** (e.g., fitness, travel, food, fashion, tech)
2. **Select platform** (Instagram, TikTok, Twitter)
3. **Click "Search"**
4. **Review results:**
   - **Stats overview** - Total found, trending count, average engagement
   - **Optimal Mix** - Top 3 high-competition hashtags
   - **Trending Now** - Currently trending hashtags
   - **Niche Opportunities** - Lower competition hashtags
   - **Full table** - All related hashtags with details
5. **Copy hashtags** - Click "Copy All" on any section

## Expected Outcome

- **10+ related hashtags** for your topic
- **Volume data** - Post count for each hashtag
- **Engagement ratings** - High, Medium, or Low
- **Competition levels** - High, Medium, or Low
- **Trending indicators** - Which hashtags are hot right now
- **Organized categories:**
  - Optimal mix for maximum reach
  - Trending tags for current visibility
  - Niche opportunities for less competition

## Hashtag Categories

### Optimal Mix
- Top 3 hashtags with high competition
- Best for maximum reach
- High volume, high engagement

### Trending Now
- Currently trending hashtags
- Growing in popularity
- Good for timely content

### Niche Opportunities
- Lower competition hashtags
- Easier to rank for
- Better for smaller accounts

## Supported Topics

- **Fitness** - Workout, gym, health hashtags
- **Travel** - Tourism, adventure, wanderlust
- **Food** - Foodie, recipes, restaurants
- **Fashion** - Style, OOTD, fashionista
- **Tech** - Technology, AI, coding, startups

## Features

- **Platform-specific** - Different recommendations per platform
- **Volume metrics** - See how popular each hashtag is
- **Engagement ratings** - Predict potential reach
- **Competition analysis** - Find easier-to-rank hashtags
- **Trending detection** - Identify hot hashtags
- **One-click copy** - Easy to use in your posts
