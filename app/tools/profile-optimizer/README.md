# Profile Optimizer

## Purpose
Analyze and optimize your social media profile bio for maximum engagement and discoverability. Get actionable tips to improve your bio's effectiveness across platforms like Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, and TikTok.

## How to Use

1. **Enter Your Current Bio:**
   - Paste or type your current social media bio in the "Current bio" field
   - The character counter shows your bio length (max 200 characters)

2. **Add Keywords:**
   - Enter relevant keywords separated by commas (e.g., "content creator, marketing, design")
   - These are keywords you want to include in your bio for discoverability

3. **Optimize:**
   - Click the "Optimise" button to analyze your bio
   - Review your optimization score and detailed analysis

4. **Review Results:**
   - **Score:** See your optimization score out of 3
   - **Analysis:** Check which elements are present (length, keywords, emojis)
   - **Tips:** Follow the improvement suggestions to enhance your bio

## Expected Outcome

- An optimization score (0-3) indicating how well-optimized your bio is
- Detailed analysis of bio length, keyword presence, and emoji usage
- Actionable tips to improve your bio's effectiveness
- Visual indicators (✓/✗) showing what's working and what needs improvement

## Features

- **Length Analysis:** Checks if your bio is within optimal character range (50-150)
- **Keyword Detection:** Verifies if your specified keywords appear in the bio
- **Emoji Detection:** Checks for emoji usage to improve engagement
- **Score System:** Provides a clear 0-3 score for quick assessment
- **Actionable Tips:** Specific suggestions for improving your bio
- **Character Counter:** Real-time character count to help you stay within limits

## Optimization Criteria

The tool evaluates your bio based on:

1. **Length (1 point):** Bio should be 50-150 characters for optimal engagement
2. **Keywords (1 point):** Bio should include your specified keywords
3. **Emojis (1 point):** Bio should include emojis for better visual appeal

## Tips

- **Optimal Length:** Aim for 50-150 characters for most platforms
- **Keywords:** Include relevant keywords naturally in your bio
- **Emojis:** Use 1-3 emojis to make your bio more engaging
- **Platform-Specific:** Different platforms have different character limits:
  - Instagram: 150 characters
  - Twitter/X: 160 characters
  - LinkedIn: 120 characters
  - TikTok: 80 characters
- **Call to Action:** Consider adding a clear call to action
- **Update Regularly:** Refresh your bio periodically to keep it current
