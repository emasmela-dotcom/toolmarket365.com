# Quote Card Generator

## What It Does
Creates beautiful quote cards for social media. Customize quotes, colors, themes, and sizes. Add logos and download as PNG images ready to post.

## How to Use

1. **Enter quote:**
   - Type or paste your quote in the text area
   - Use line breaks for multi-line quotes

2. **Add author (optional):**
   - Enter author name
   - Will appear as "— Author Name" below quote

3. **Choose theme:**
   - **Minimal** - Clean, simple design
   - **Brush** - Artistic brush strokes overlay
   - **Neon** - Neon border effect
   - **Retro** - Vintage yellow tint
   - **Dark** - Dark background

4. **Customize colors:**
   - **Text colour** - Choose quote text color
   - **Background colour** - Choose card background

5. **Select size:**
   - **Instagram 1080×1080** - Square posts
   - **Twitter 1200×675** - Twitter cards
   - **Story 1080×1920** - Instagram/TikTok stories

6. **Add logo (optional):**
   - Click "Choose Image" to upload logo/avatar
   - Appears in top-right corner

7. **Download:**
   - Click "Download PNG" to save image
   - Ready to post on social media
