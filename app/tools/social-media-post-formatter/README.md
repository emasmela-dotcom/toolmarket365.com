# Social Media Post Formatter

## What It Does
Formats your social media content for different platforms with character limits, text styling (bold, italic, underline), emoji picker, and AI hashtag suggestions.

## How to Use

1. **Enter your content** in the text area
2. **Select your platform** (Twitter/X, Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook, TikTok)
3. **Format your text:**
   - Select text and click Bold, Italic, or Underline buttons
   - Click emojis to insert them
   - Use "🪄 AI hashtags" to auto-generate hashtags from your content
4. **Check character count** - automatically adjusts based on platform limits
5. **Copy the output** using the "Copy output" button

## Expected Outcome

- **Formatted text** ready to paste into your social media platform
- **Platform-specific character limits** enforced (Twitter: 280, Instagram: 2200, etc.)
- **Styled text** using Unicode characters (works on most platforms)
- **Hashtags** automatically generated from your content keywords
- **Character counter** showing used/limit for your selected platform

## Platform Limits

- Twitter/X: 280 characters
- Instagram: 2,200 characters
- LinkedIn: 3,000 characters
- Facebook: 63,206 characters
- TikTok: 2,200 characters

## Tips

- Use bold/italic for emphasis (works on most platforms)
- AI hashtags extracts 4+ letter words and capitalizes them
- Preview shows exactly what will be copied
