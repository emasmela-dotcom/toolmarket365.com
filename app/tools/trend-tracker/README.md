# Trend Tracker

## Purpose
Monitor trending topics, keywords, and hashtags in your niche over time. Track weekly trend scores to identify rising, stable, or falling topics that can inform your content strategy.

## How to Use

1. **Enter Keyword:**
   - Type a keyword, topic, or hashtag you want to track
   - Examples: "AI", "sustainable fashion", "#wellness", "remote work"

2. **Track:**
   - Click the "Track" button
   - View weekly trend scores for the past 4 weeks

3. **Analyze Results:**
   - See trend scores for each week (W1, W2, W3, W4)
   - Check the overall trend direction (Rising, Falling, or Stable)
   - Review visual progress bars for each week
   - Understand score ranges and what they mean

## Expected Outcome

- Weekly trend scores (0-100) for the past 4 weeks
- Visual progress bars showing trend strength
- Overall trend direction indicator (Rising/Falling/Stable)
- Color-coded scores for quick assessment
- Clear visualization of how trends change over time

## Features

- **Weekly Tracking:** See trends over 4 weeks
- **Score System:** 0-100 scale for trend strength
- **Visual Progress Bars:** Easy-to-read trend visualization
- **Trend Direction:** Quick indicator of rising/falling/stable trends
- **Color Coding:**
  - Green (70+): High trend
  - Yellow (50-69): Moderate trend
  - Red (Below 50): Low trend

## Score Interpretation

- **70-100:** High trend - Strong interest, good time to create content
- **50-69:** Moderate trend - Steady interest, consider creating content
- **Below 50:** Low trend - Declining interest, may want to wait

## Trend Directions

- **📈 Rising:** Trend is increasing over time (last week significantly higher than first)
- **📉 Falling:** Trend is decreasing over time (last week significantly lower than first)
- **➡️ Stable:** Trend is relatively consistent (no significant change)

## Tips

- **Track Multiple Keywords:** Compare different topics to find the best opportunities
- **Regular Monitoring:** Track keywords weekly to catch trends early
- **Content Planning:** Use rising trends to plan timely content
- **Avoid Declining Trends:** Steer clear of topics that are losing interest
- **Combine with Other Tools:** Use with Content Idea Generator for trend-based content
- **Niche-Specific:** Track keywords relevant to your specific niche or industry
- **Timing:** Create content when trends are rising for maximum engagement

## Use Cases

- Identify trending topics in your niche
- Plan content around rising trends
- Avoid topics that are declining
- Stay ahead of the curve with early trend detection
- Benchmark your content topics against trend data
- Discover new content opportunities
