# Viral Content Predictor

AI-powered tool to predict your content's viral potential before you post.

## Features

- **Viral Score Prediction**: Get a 0-100 score predicting your content's viral potential
- **Emotional Analysis**: Analyzes emotional triggers, sentiment, empathy, and shareability
- **Visual Analysis**: Evaluates visual elements, color psychology, composition, and attention-grabbing factors
- **Timing Analysis**: Determines optimal posting times based on trends, seasonality, and audience activity
- **Competitor Analysis**: Compares against competitor content and identifies opportunities
- **Hashtag Analysis**: Recommends effective hashtags for maximum reach
- **Engagement Predictions**: Forecasts expected likes, comments, shares, and views
- **Risk Factors & Opportunities**: Identifies areas for improvement and optimization

## How It Works

1. Enter your content text/caption
2. Select your target platform (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Twitter, LinkedIn)
3. Choose your media type (video, image, carousel, reel)
4. Get instant analysis with:
   - Viral potential score (0-100)
   - Predicted engagement metrics
   - Optimal posting time
   - Recommended hashtags
   - Emotional breakdown
   - Risk factors and opportunities

## Use Cases

- **Content Creators**: Test content before posting to maximize viral potential
- **Social Media Managers**: Optimize content strategy with data-driven insights
- **Marketing Teams**: Predict content performance and allocate resources effectively
- **Brands**: Ensure content aligns with viral trends and audience preferences
